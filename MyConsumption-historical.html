Rollover

Rollover

With Rollover, you can use the balance of your previous month's franchise, for the current month.

For example, if your plan has 20Gb per month and you only used 10Gb, the next month you will have 20Gb of your plan and 10Gb that you did not use in the previous month.

But attention: If you do not use these 10Gb that "left" it will not accumulate for the next period.
